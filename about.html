Who We Are

CAL Trans is a South African transport and logistics company focused on providing safe, reliable and efficient freight solutions. Our experienced team understands that every delivery is important, which is why we pride ourselves on professionalism, punctuality and exceptional customer service.

Whether transporting goods across provinces or managing logistics for businesses, our goal is to provide dependable transport services that keep our clients moving forward.
