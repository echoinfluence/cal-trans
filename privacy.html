Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At CAL Trans, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect information obtained through our website and our transport services. By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us or request a quotation:

  • Full Name
  • Company Name
  • Email Address
  • Telephone Number
  • Collection and Delivery Locations
  • Freight or Transport Information
  • Messages and Enquiries
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We may also automatically collect technical information including:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Operating System
  • Device Information
  • Website Pages Visited
  • Date and Time of Access

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Respond to enquiries.
  • Prepare transport quotations.
  • Provide transport and logistics services.
  • Communicate regarding bookings and deliveries.
  • Improve our website and customer experience.
  • Maintain business records.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

CAL Trans does not sell, rent or trade your personal information. Your information may only be shared where necessary with:

  • Transport partners or subcontractors involved in delivering our services.
  • IT or website service providers.
  • Government or regulatory authorities where required by law.

Any third party receiving information is expected to maintain appropriate security measures.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, misuse or disclosure. Although every effort is made to secure your information, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Data Retention

Personal information will only be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide our services.
  • Respond to enquiries.
  • Maintain business records.
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Understand website traffic.
  • Improve website performance.
  • Enhance user experience.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings if you wish.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Microsoft Forms
  • Google Maps
  • Microsoft 365

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights Under POPIA

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion where legally permitted.
  • Object to certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this Policy

CAL Trans may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page together with the updated revision date.
